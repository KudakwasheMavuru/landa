About Us

The Story...

Born in 1979 in Rwanda, he grew up in a home with a composer and pianist father, a music lover, and a nurse mother devoted to her work and especially to her family. On leaving secondary school, he decided to take computer management courses at the ISFCE in Brussels. By taking these courses, he discovered computer-assisted music and began to compose and create music on his own.

What we offer

Our Services

Instrumental music composition

Composition of instrumental music in urban style and put out for disposal to artists, television channels and radio stations through databases available via: digital platforms (Spotify, Beatstars, Tunecore, … ), the website of Landa Production (in progress) and the passage in the studio of an artist

Custom composition (targeted)

Music creation according to the needs and requirements of the client (artist, television channel and radio station) with the aim of obtaining a personalized result through bilateral collaboration.

Recording, mixing and mastering

The customer chooses the beat he wants and puts his voice and/or additional instruments on it. Once everything is recorded, Landa Production mixes and masters the product to arrive at a finished product ready for exploitation.

Mixing and mastering can also be done on tracks sent to us by the customer. The customer can send us a beat via our platform to mix and master according to his needs and wishes.

Development and management of artists

Landa Production accompanies its talents through a personalized follow-up in relation to the artist and his universe. Vocal coaching, writing and artistic direction sessions are made available to artists in order to be autonomous and ready to work on tracks in collaboration with the team. The artist's career is managed 360° by Landa Production.

Creation of content for digital media

Digital media is a potential source of revenue that must be exploited to its maximum by creating quality content. Landa Production will create this quality content (audio, video and graphics) in order to attract a maximum of potential customers (TV and digital radio, youtubers, influencers, ...)

Production videoclips

Creation of video clips according to the musical universe and the budget of the artist.

Graphism

Creation of graphics on request for customers (banners, logos, covers, cd covers, ...)

Website design

Website design in collaboration with the artist by listening to his needs so that the artist can make his music available to his audience.

Event management

Organization and technical support of events such as concerts, tours, album promotion, ...
